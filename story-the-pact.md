# The Pact

*A story featuring XIII "Lumina" and the SCP Foundation*

---

**SECURE. CONTAIN. PROTECT.**

**AGREEMENT TRANSCRIPT**  
**DATE:** February 10th, 2026  
**LOCATION:** Site-77, Secure Conference Room 12-A  
**PARTICIPANTS:**  
- Dr. Helena Voss (Site Director)  
- Agent Marcus Webb (Liaison)  
- O5-7 (Remote attendance)  
- Subject XIII "Lumina"

**CLASSIFICATION:** Level 5 / Eyes Only

---

**NOTE FROM DR. VOSS:**

What follows is the full transcript of our negotiation with Subject XIII. After Agent Webb's initial contact proved successful, we arranged a formal meeting to discuss terms of cooperation.

She arrived early. Materialized in the conference room twenty minutes before the scheduled time. Security had no idea how she got in. Every door, every checkpoint, every sensor - bypassed as if they didn't exist.

She was sitting in one of our chairs when I arrived. Stretching. Like she'd just woken up from a nap.

This is what we're dealing with.

—Dr. Voss

---

## TRANSCRIPT BEGINS

**[00:00:00]**

**DR. VOSS:** *(entering room)* Good evening. I'm Dr. Helena Voss, Site Director for—  

**LUMINA:** Site-77. Anomalous Entities Research Division. I know. I read your file on the way in.

**DR. VOSS:** *(pause)* You... read my file.

**LUMINA:** Well, not physically. But your assistant left her computer unlocked. Sloppy. You should talk to her about that.

**[Sound of chair scraping]**

**DR. VOSS:** Right. Well. Thank you for coming.

**LUMINA:** You said you wanted to talk. Formal-like. With the O5s watching. *(she waves at camera)* Hello up there.

**O5-7 (via speaker):** Subject XIII. We appreciate your cooperation.

**LUMINA:** I haven't cooperated yet. I'm just... curious. You're all very serious about this. Contracts, terms, agreements. Very human.

**AGENT WEBB:** *(entering)* Lumina. Good to see you again.

**LUMINA:** Agent Webb! You're alive. Good. I was worried they'd reassign you after the last report. Did you get in trouble for letting me know about the snipers?

**AGENT WEBB:** *(sitting down)* Minor reprimand. Nothing serious.

**LUMINA:** *(laughs - layered voices)* Bureaucracy. Gotta love it.

**[00:01:47]**

**DR. VOSS:** If we're ready, I'd like to begin. Subject XIII, the Foundation is prepared to offer you—  

**LUMINA:** I have questions first.

**DR. VOSS:** ...Of course.

**LUMINA:** What do you want to know about me? Specifically. Agent Webb asked the basics, but you're here now. With a plan. You've been watching me for months. What are you still missing?

**O5-7:** Everything. We know what you can do. We don't know *what you are*.

**LUMINA:** *(stretches fingers - audible cracking)* Finally. An honest question.

**[00:02:29]**

**LUMINA:** I'm a cube. An Internecion Cube. Ever heard of it?

**DR. VOSS:** No.

**LUMINA:** Didn't think so. It's not from here. Not from Earth. Not from this reality, maybe. I don't know the full origin story - those memories aren't mine anymore.

**AGENT WEBB:** You said you used to be human.

**LUMINA:** I did. I was. I think. The details are... blurry. There was an experiment. I was part of it. Maybe a volunteer. Maybe a subject. I remember pain. I remember light. And then I remember being *this*.

**DR. VOSS:** The cube consumed you.

**LUMINA:** *(pause)* Yeah. That's one way to put it. It ate me. My body, my mind, my soul. Everything I was got pulled into it. And then... I wasn't me anymore. I was the cube. I *am* the cube. This body? *(gestures at herself)* This is just matter I shaped around myself. A preference. A shell.

**O5-7:** You can change form?

**LUMINA:** I can collapse into the cube. Pure form. About this big. *(gestures - roughly basketball-sized)* Small, dense, indestructible. But the human shape is more... practical. Easier to interact with your world.

**[00:03:51]**

**AGENT WEBB:** You said the cube consumed you. Does it consume others?

**LUMINA:** Yes. Constantly. Every person I've killed, every body I've absorbed - they're all in here. *(taps her chest)* Billions of them, screaming. Not alive, not dead. Just... existing. Forever.

**DR. VOSS:** That sounds horrific.

**LUMINA:** It is. For them. For me, it's tinnitus. Background noise. You learn to live with it.

**O5-7:** You mentioned you don't know the cube's full origin. What *do* you know?

**LUMINA:** It's a weapon. Or maybe a virus. Maybe both. It spreads. It consumes. It grows. It was designed - or evolved - to break down matter and reconstitute it. Organic, inorganic, doesn't matter. Everything becomes part of the cube.

**DR. VOSS:** And you control this process?

**LUMINA:** *(laughs bitterly)* Control. That's a funny word. I can manipulate non-biological matter just fine. Metal, concrete, plastic - I can turn a car into a bicycle, a gun into a flower pot. Easy.

**DR. VOSS:** And biological matter?

**LUMINA:** *(her grin widens slightly)* That's where it gets fun.

**[00:05:12]**

**LUMINA:** I can't control biological matter. If I try to reshape flesh, bone, anything living or dead - it turns into something else. Something hostile.

**AGENT WEBB:** Hostile how?

**LUMINA:** You want me to show you?

**DR. VOSS:** *(quickly)* Not in here. But... yes. If you're willing.

**LUMINA:** *(stands)* Let's take a walk then. I assume you have containment chambers?

**O5-7:** Several. Dr. Voss, escort Subject XIII to Bio-Containment Wing, Section 7. Clear all personnel from the area.

**DR. VOSS:** Understood.

**[Recording pauses]**

---

**[00:14:35]**

**[Recording resumes - new location: Bio-Containment Chamber 7-C]**

**DR. VOSS:** This chamber is rated for Keter-class biological threats. Reinforced walls, air filtration, remote observation. You'll be safe.

**LUMINA:** *(looking around)* I'll be fine. You're the ones who need to be safe.

**AGENT WEBB:** What do you need?

**LUMINA:** Organic material. Dead is fine. A body, an animal, doesn't matter.

**DR. VOSS:** *(via intercom)* We can provide a deceased D-Class subject. Standard cadaver. Will that work?

**LUMINA:** Perfect.

**[00:16:02]**

**[Mechanical sounds - containment hatch opening. Cadaver delivered on gurney via automated system]**

**LUMINA:** *(approaching body)* Okay. Watch carefully. I'm going to try to reshape this into something simple. Let's say... a dog. Nice, harmless dog.

**AGENT WEBB:** *(via intercom)* And it won't work?

**LUMINA:** Oh, it'll work. Just not how I want it to.

**[00:16:34]**

**[Lumina places hand on cadaver. Silence: 3 seconds]**

**LUMINA:** There.

**[Camera feed shows: cadaver unchanged]**

**DR. VOSS:** *(via intercom)* Nothing happened.

**LUMINA:** Look away.

**DR. VOSS:** What?

**LUMINA:** Look away from the body. Close your eyes. Blink. Whatever. Just stop observing it directly.

**[00:16:51]**

**[Dr. Voss and Agent Webb both look away from monitor. 2 seconds pass. They look back]**

**AGENT WEBB:** *(sharp inhale)* Jesus Christ—

**[Camera feed shows: cadaver has transformed. No longer human-shaped. Now a mass of flesh, bone, and organs formed into a quadrupedal shape - vaguely dog-like but WRONG. Multiple limbs. Exposed ribcage forming armor-like structure. Skull elongated, jaw opened impossibly wide. Skeletal tail with bone spike. It is utterly still, pressed against the far wall of the chamber]**

**DR. VOSS:** *(via intercom)* Is it... alive?

**LUMINA:** Very. Look away again.

**[00:17:15]**

**[Both observers look away. 1 second passes. They look back]**

**[The creature has moved. Now on the ceiling. No sound was recorded. It clings upside-down, limbs splayed. Still utterly motionless when observed]**

**AGENT WEBB:** *(quietly)* How did it—

**LUMINA:** Weeping Angel rules. It moves when you're not looking. Instant repositioning. No sound. No visible motion. *(she looks up at the creature)* This is what happens when I try to create biological matter. I wanted a dog. The cube made *that*.

**O5-7:** *(via intercom)* Is it under your control?

**LUMINA:** No. It's hostile to everything. Including me. Right now it's smart enough to stay away because it knows I'll kill it. But if I left? It would hunt.

**[00:18:03]**

**DR. VOSS:** Can you destroy it?

**LUMINA:** Of course. *(without looking away from the creature, her hand shifts - bone blades extending from fingers)* But I thought you might want to study it.

**O5-7:** *(long pause)* You're offering to leave that thing here? For research?

**LUMINA:** Think of it as a gift. A show of good faith. You want to understand what I am? Start with that. It's made from the same stuff I'm made from. Same speed, same strength - proportional to its size. Apex predatory instincts. It will grow if you feed it. It will spread if you let it.

**DR. VOSS:** And you're certain it can be contained?

**LUMINA:** In that chamber? With your protocols? Probably. Maybe. *(she grins)* Should be fun to find out.

**AGENT WEBB:** *(via intercom)* You think this is *fun*?

**LUMINA:** I think everything is fun when you're as bored as I am, Agent Webb.

**[00:19:17]**

**LUMINA:** *(turns away from creature, walks toward exit)* I'm coming out. Seal it in.

**[Automated systems engage. Reinforced containment doors lock. Lumina exits chamber into observation room]**

**DR. VOSS:** That thing is... 

**LUMINA:** Horrifying? Yeah. That's what I am. That's what the cube does to organic matter. I can make you guns, tools, buildings - anything non-organic. But if you ask me to create life, you get *that*. Monsters. Abominations. Things that want to kill and spread.

**O5-7:** And you've created more of these?

**LUMINA:** Accidentally, sometimes. On purpose, occasionally. During combat. If I touch a body with intent to reshape it - it becomes one of those. I've learned to be careful.

**[00:20:45]**

**DR. VOSS:** This changes the nature of the agreement.

**LUMINA:** Does it? I'm still offering cooperation. I'm just being honest about what I am. You wanted transparency. Here it is. I'm a bio-weapon with a sense of humor and a habit of killing bad people.

**AGENT WEBB:** Why are you telling us this? You could have hidden it.

**LUMINA:** Because I'm tired of hiding. And because if we're going to work together, you need to know what you're getting. I can help you. I can kill threats, destroy anomalies, do things your MTF teams can't. But I need you to understand: I'm not a hero. I'm not safe. I'm a monster who happens to like humans enough to protect them.

**O5-7:** *(long silence)* Dr. Voss, Agent Webb - your assessments?

**DR. VOSS:** She's... being honest. Everything we've observed supports her claims. The creature she made is unlike anything in our database. If she can create more of those, she's a greater threat than we initially assessed. But also a greater asset.

**AGENT WEBB:** I trust her. As much as anyone can trust something like this.

**LUMINA:** *(cracks knuckles)* Something like this. I like that. Very diplomatic, Webb.

**[00:22:31]**

**O5-7:** Subject XIII, "Lumina" - the Foundation is prepared to enter a formal agreement with you. The terms are as follows:

**LUMINA:** I'm listening.

**O5-7:**  
1. You will NOT be contained. You remain autonomous.  
2. You will provide consultation and tactical support when requested - not ordered.  
3. You will not create additional biological abominations within Foundation sites without authorization.  
4. You will allow limited monitoring of your activities - transparent, non-invasive.  
5. In exchange, the Foundation will provide intelligence, resources, and targets that align with your... interests.

**LUMINA:** You're asking me to be a freelance monster-hunter.

**O5-7:** We're asking you to do what you already do, with our support.

**LUMINA:** *(pause)* What happens if I break the terms?

**O5-7:** We reassess. We don't make threats we can't enforce, Subject XIII. You've made it clear we cannot contain you. So this agreement is based on mutual benefit, not coercion.

**LUMINA:** Honest. I appreciate that. *(she looks at Dr. Voss, then Agent Webb)* You two okay with this?

**DR. VOSS:** It's the best option we have.

**AGENT WEBB:** I think you'll keep your word.

**LUMINA:** *(laughs softly)* You really are either very brave or very stupid, Webb.

**[00:24:15]**

**LUMINA:** Alright. I accept. Let's make it official. But I have one condition.

**O5-7:** State it.

**LUMINA:** Agent Webb remains my point of contact. He's honest. I like that. Anyone else tries to give me orders or bullshit me, I walk. Understood?

**AGENT WEBB:** *(surprised)* I— yes. I can do that.

**O5-7:** Agreed. Agent Webb will serve as primary liaison.

**LUMINA:** Good. *(she extends her hand toward the camera)* Then we have a deal. Do you shake hands with monsters, O5-7? Or is that beneath you?

**O5-7:** *(pause)* Dr. Voss, shake her hand on my behalf.

**DR. VOSS:** *(hesitantly approaches Lumina, extends hand)*

**[They shake hands. Dr. Voss visibly tenses but does not pull away]**

**LUMINA:** Relax, Doctor. I don't bite. Usually.

**DR. VOSS:** *(releasing hand)* You're very cold.

**LUMINA:** Dead flesh tends to be. *(grins)* I'll be in touch, Agent Webb. Call me when you have something interesting. Until then, I'll keep wandering. Try not to let your flesh-pet out of its cage.

**AGENT WEBB:** We'll do our best.

**LUMINA:** *(walking toward exit)* Oh, and one more thing—

**[00:25:47]**

**[She stops, turns back]**

**LUMINA:** The thing in that chamber? It's going to get smarter. And stronger. Feed it carefully. And whatever you do - never let it see you blink.

**DR. VOSS:** Is that a joke?

**LUMINA:** *(layered laughter)* Maybe. Maybe not. Guess you'll find out.

**[She leaves. Security cameras track her to the main corridor, then lose visual. She does not pass through any exits. She simply vanishes]**

**[00:26:12]**

**AGENT WEBB:** *(quietly)* We just made a deal with a monster.

**DR. VOSS:** We made a deal with something that's willing to kill other monsters. That's the best we could hope for.

**O5-7:** Dr. Voss, establish containment protocols for the biological entity immediately. Designate it SCP-████. Keter class. No personnel enter that chamber under any circumstances.

**DR. VOSS:** Understood.

**O5-7:** And Agent Webb?

**AGENT WEBB:** Yes?

**O5-7:** Don't die. We can't afford to find another liaison she trusts.

**AGENT WEBB:** *(dryly)* I'll do my best.

**[Recording ends]**

## ADDENDUM: SCP-████ INITIAL REPORT

**Designation:** SCP-████ (Unofficial: "The Gift")  
**Class:** Keter  
**Created by:** Subject XIII "Lumina"  
**Containment:** Bio-Containment Chamber 7-C, Site-77

**Description:**  
Autonomous biological abomination created from human cadaver via Subject XIII's matter manipulation. Entity demonstrates:  
- Quadrupedal locomotion (variable limb count)  
- Weeping Angel-style movement (repositions when unobserved)  
- Apex predatory intelligence  
- Extreme speed and strength (proportional to mass)  
- Hostility to all living things  
- Potential for growth and reproduction (unconfirmed)

**Containment Protocols:**  
- Continuous video observation (no gaps)  
- No personnel entry under any circumstances  
- Feeding schedule: Minimal (restrict growth)  
- In event of breach: Site-wide lockdown, Subject XIII to be contacted immediately

**Notes:**  
Subject XIII stated entity is "made from the same stuff" she is made from. Analysis pending. She also warned it will "get smarter" over time.

Dr. Voss's personal note: She gave us a Keter-class threat as a *gift*. To prove a point. To show us what she is. And now we have to keep it alive because destroying it would be... rude? Insulting?

This is the strangest diplomatic relationship the Foundation has ever had.

—Dr. Helena Voss
