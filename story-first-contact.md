# First Contact

*A short story featuring XIII "Lumina"*

---

**SECURE. CONTAIN. PROTECT.**

**INTERNAL MEMORANDUM**  
**FROM:** Dr. Helena Voss, Site-77 Anomalous Entities Research Division  
**TO:** O5 Council  
**RE:** Subject XIII / "Lumina" - Initial Contact Report  
**CLASSIFICATION:** Level 4/Restricted

---

The following is a transcript of Agent Marcus Webb's encounter with Subject XIII, designated "Lumina," during our first official contact attempt. Agent Webb was equipped with standard recording equipment and authorized to establish peaceful communication.

We... were not prepared.

—Dr. Voss

---

## TRANSCRIPT BEGINS

**[00:00:00]**

**AGENT WEBB:** Recording active. This is Agent Marcus Webb, Foundation ID 7745-Theta. Date is February 2nd, 2026. Time is 21:47 hours. I am approaching the last known location of Subject XIII.

**[Background noise: footsteps on concrete, distant traffic]**

**AGENT WEBB:** Subject was tracked to an abandoned industrial district on the north side of the city. No civilian presence detected within a two-block radius. Proceeding with caution.

**[00:02:34]**

**AGENT WEBB:** Visual contact established. Subject is... standing on a rooftop. Four stories up. Just standing there, looking out over the city. I'm going to attempt approach from ground level. Activating loudspeaker.

**[Sound of electronic feedback]**

**AGENT WEBB:** *(via loudspeaker)* This is Agent Webb with the SCP Foundation. I'm here to talk. I'm unarmed and I'm alone. I'd like to discuss a mutually beneficial arrangement.

**[Silence: 8 seconds]**

**AGENT WEBB:** *(muttered)* Come on, don't make me—

**[Sudden loud CRACK - concrete breaking]**

**AGENT WEBB:** Jesus Christ—

**[Recording picks up heavy impact, dust and debris sounds]**

**UNKNOWN VOICE (LUMINA):** You shouldn't swear. It's unprofessional.

**[00:03:12]**

**AGENT WEBB:** *(coughing)* You—you just dropped four stories.

**LUMINA:** Three and a half, technically. The ground floor is partially below street level.

**AGENT WEBB:** *(breathing steadying)* Right. Um. Thank you for... coming down. I'm Agent Webb. I'd like to talk, if that's okay.

**LUMINA:** You said that already. Through your little speaker toy.

**AGENT WEBB:** I did. Yes. Can I... can I see you? It's hard to see in this light.

**[Sound of footsteps - heavy boots on concrete]**

**LUMINA:** Better?

**[00:03:45]**

**AGENT WEBB:** *(pause)* You're... tall.

**LUMINA:** Two meters, four centimeters. You're observant. That must be why they sent you.

**AGENT WEBB:** I— *(clears throat)* I was told you might be willing to speak with us. The Foundation has been tracking your movements for the past three months. We know you've been... intervening. Stopping human trafficking operations, cartels, that sort of thing.

**LUMINA:** You make it sound noble. I was bored.

**AGENT WEBB:** Bored?

**LUMINA:** Do you know what eternity feels like, Agent Webb?

**AGENT WEBB:** I... can't say I do.

**LUMINA:** Then you're lucky. *(pause)* What does the Foundation want?

**AGENT WEBB:** We want to understand you. What you are. Where you came from. And ideally... we'd like to establish a working relationship.

**[Sound: sharp CRACK]**

**AGENT WEBB:** What was—

**LUMINA:** Neck. Sorry. Habit. You were saying?

**AGENT WEBB:** *(shakily)* A working relationship. You seem to have... similar goals to ours. Protecting humanity from threats. We have resources, intelligence networks, targets that might interest you—

**LUMINA:** You want to aim me at things.

**AGENT WEBB:** I—that's not—

**LUMINA:** It's fine. I don't mind being honest if you don't. You're scared of me. Your Foundation has no idea what I am or how to stop me, so you're here with your recording equipment and your nervous smile, hoping I'll agree to play nice.

**AGENT WEBB:** *(long pause)* ...Yes. That's accurate.

**[00:05:23]**

**LUMINA:** I like you, Agent Webb. You didn't lie just now.

**AGENT WEBB:** Thank you, I think.

**LUMINA:** Most people lie when they're afraid. They tell themselves it'll save them. But you just... told the truth. That takes guts.

**AGENT WEBB:** Or stupidity.

**LUMINA:** *(sound that might be laughter - layered, multiple voices)* Maybe both.

**AGENT WEBB:** Can I ask you something?

**LUMINA:** You're going to anyway.

**AGENT WEBB:** What are you?

**[Silence: 12 seconds]**

**LUMINA:** I don't know anymore.

**AGENT WEBB:** You... don't know?

**LUMINA:** I used to be human. I think. The memories are... blurry. Then there was the cube. The fusion. The... *(pause)* Do you know what it's like to have a billion voices screaming in your head, Agent Webb?

**AGENT WEBB:** No.

**LUMINA:** Every person I've consumed. Every soul absorbed into the cube. They're all still in there. Not alive, not dead. Just... existing. Screaming. Always screaming.

**AGENT WEBB:** That sounds... terrible.

**LUMINA:** You get used to it. Like tinnitus. Eventually it's just background noise.

**[00:06:51]**

**AGENT WEBB:** The Foundation could help. We have containment specialists, psychological—

**LUMINA:** Containment. *(voice shifts - harder edge)* You think you can contain me?

**AGENT WEBB:** I didn't mean—

**LUMINA:** No, it's fine. It's what you do. Secure, contain, protect. I've heard the motto. Very catchy.

**AGENT WEBB:** We're not trying to imprison you. We just want—

**LUMINA:** To know I won't become a problem. To make sure I stay in my lane. To have a file somewhere that says "Subject XIII - Status: Cooperative."

**AGENT WEBB:** *(quietly)* Yes.

**[Silence: 6 seconds]**

**LUMINA:** At least you're honest.

**[Sound: footsteps approaching]**

**AGENT WEBB:** Are you—what are you—

**LUMINA:** Relax. I'm not going to hurt you. I'm just... curious.

**[00:07:45]**

**AGENT WEBB:** Curious about what?

**LUMINA:** Why you're not running. Everyone else does.

**AGENT WEBB:** I'm... terrified, if I'm being honest.

**LUMINA:** I know. I can hear your heartbeat from here. But you're still standing. Still talking. Why?

**AGENT WEBB:** Because someone has to. And because... *(pause)* because you saved those people. The trafficking victims. The kids in that cartel compound last week. You didn't have to do that.

**LUMINA:** I told you. I was bored.

**AGENT WEBB:** I don't believe you.

**[Silence: 9 seconds]**

**LUMINA:** *(softer, layered voices slightly less pronounced)* You should.

**AGENT WEBB:** I think... I think part of you still cares. About humans. About what happens to them.

**LUMINA:** Careful, Agent Webb. You're getting philosophical. That's dangerous when you're standing three feet from something that could kill you in a dozen different ways.

**AGENT WEBB:** Is that a threat?

**LUMINA:** No. Just a fact. *(pause)* But you're right. Sort of. I don't... hate humans. Individually, you're all very fragile and very boring. But as a concept? Humanity? You're... interesting. Entertaining. Like watching ants build colonies, except some of the ants write poetry and some of them build nuclear weapons.

**AGENT WEBB:** That's... one way to put it.

**[00:09:12]**

**LUMINA:** Can I ask you something now?

**AGENT WEBB:** Of course.

**LUMINA:** Why does the Foundation care? About me specifically. You deal with world-ending threats. Reality benders. Entities that could crack the planet in half. I'm just... wandering around. Killing bad people occasionally. Why does that warrant an O5-approved contact mission?

**AGENT WEBB:** How did you—

**LUMINA:** Your heart rate spiked when you said "working relationship." That's not field agent vocabulary. That's bureaucratic speak. Someone high up approved this. So I'll ask again: why do they care?

**AGENT WEBB:** *(long pause)* Because you're an unknown variable. And unknowns make the Foundation nervous.

**LUMINA:** Mmm. Honest again. I appreciate that.

**AGENT WEBB:** We've been monitoring anomalies for decades. We have protocols, containment procedures, response teams. But you? You don't fit any of our classifications. You're not hostile, but you're not cooperative. You're immensely powerful, but you don't seem interested in conquest or destruction. You're... an outlier.

**LUMINA:** And outliers are dangerous.

**AGENT WEBB:** In our experience? Yes.

**[00:10:34]**

**LUMINA:** So what now? You file your report, tell the O5s that Subject XIII is "potentially cooperative but unpredictable," and then what? Do they send more agents? Do they try to contain me anyway? Do they—

**AGENT WEBB:** I don't know. That's above my pay grade.

**LUMINA:** *(sound of laughter - multiple voices overlapping)* Above your pay grade. I like that. You're very human, Agent Webb.

**AGENT WEBB:** I'll take that as a compliment.

**LUMINA:** You should. *(pause)* Tell your bosses this: I'm not interested in being contained. I'm not interested in being a weapon. But I'm also not interested in being a threat. I just... exist. And occasionally, when I'm bored enough, I kill people who deserve it.

**AGENT WEBB:** That's... not exactly reassuring.

**LUMINA:** It's the truth. And you like the truth, don't you?

**AGENT WEBB:** I do.

**LUMINA:** Then here's another truth: your Foundation is afraid of me because they can't control me. And they should be. Because I could walk into Site-77 right now and there's nothing you could do to stop me.

**[00:11:47]**

**AGENT WEBB:** *(voice tight)* Are you... planning to do that?

**LUMINA:** No. Like I said, I'm not interested in being a threat. But I need your bosses to understand something: I'm not a dog you can leash. I'm not a tool you can use. I'm... something else. Something in between. And if they try to force the issue—if they send strike teams or containment units or whatever else they're planning—they will lose. Badly.

**AGENT WEBB:** I'll... relay that message.

**LUMINA:** Good. *(pause)* You're shaking.

**AGENT WEBB:** I'm aware.

**LUMINA:** I'm sorry. I don't mean to scare you. It's just... hard. Looking like this. Being what I am. Everyone's always afraid.

**AGENT WEBB:** I'm... I'm still here.

**LUMINA:** You are. *(softer)* Thank you for that.

**[Silence: 8 seconds]**

**[00:12:39]**

**AGENT WEBB:** Can I... ask one more thing?

**LUMINA:** Sure.

**AGENT WEBB:** What do you want? Not what the Foundation wants, not what humanity needs. What do *you* want?

**[Silence: 15 seconds]**

**LUMINA:** I don't know. Purpose, maybe. Something to fill the hours. A reason to exist that isn't just... wandering and waiting for something interesting to happen.

**AGENT WEBB:** The Foundation could give you that. Purpose, I mean. We have missions, objectives, threats that need addressing. You could—

**LUMINA:** Make myself useful?

**AGENT WEBB:** I was going to say "make a difference."

**LUMINA:** *(pause)* You're good at this. Talking. Negotiating. Do they train you for this, or is it natural talent?

**AGENT WEBB:** Bit of both, I think.

**LUMINA:** Hmm. *(sound: sharp CRACK)* Sorry. Neck again.

**AGENT WEBB:** It's... fine.

**[00:14:01]**

**LUMINA:** Tell you what, Agent Webb. I'll think about it. Your Foundation's offer. The "working relationship." But I'm not making any promises. And if they try to force my hand—

**AGENT WEBB:** They won't. I'll make sure of it.

**LUMINA:** You don't have that authority.

**AGENT WEBB:** No. But I'll try anyway.

**LUMINA:** *(laughter - softer this time)* You're either very brave or very stupid.

**AGENT WEBB:** You said that before. I'm starting to think it's the latter.

**LUMINA:** Probably. *(pause)* You should go now. Your backup team is getting nervous. They're positioned three blocks east, rifles trained on me. Tell them to stand down before someone does something unfortunate.

**AGENT WEBB:** How did you—

**LUMINA:** I can hear their heartbeats. And smell the gun oil. You really thought I wouldn't notice?

**AGENT WEBB:** That was... precautionary.

**LUMINA:** Of course it was. *(pause)* I'm not angry. I understand. But next time, just send one person. Honestly is more effective than snipers.

**AGENT WEBB:** I'll... pass that along.

**[00:15:23]**

**LUMINA:** Go, Agent Webb. Write your report. Tell your bosses I'm not a threat unless they make me one. And maybe... maybe we can talk again sometime. When you're not quite so terrified.

**AGENT WEBB:** I'd like that. I think.

**LUMINA:** Me too. *(pause)* You're a good human, Agent Webb. Try not to let your job kill that.

**AGENT WEBB:** I'll do my best.

**[Sound: heavy footsteps receding]**

**AGENT WEBB:** *(muttered)* Jesus Christ. *(louder)* This is Agent Webb. Contact established. Subject is non-hostile. Repeat, non-hostile. Stand down. All units stand down.

**[Recording continues for 3 minutes of silence before terminating]**

**ADDENDUM - Dr. Voss's Notes:**

Agent Webb's recording raises more questions than answers. Subject XIII—"Lumina"—displays clear intelligence, self-awareness, and what appears to be genuine emotional complexity. She is not a mindless entity or a simple anomaly. She is... something else.

The concerning elements:
1. She detected our backup team from three blocks away
2. She demonstrated awareness of Foundation protocols and hierarchy
3. She made it clear that containment is not an option
4. She expressed interest in potential cooperation, but on *her* terms

Recommendation: Establish Subject XIII as a Class-Thaumiel asset - uncontained, but potentially cooperative. Further contact missions approved, but only with minimal personnel and full transparency. Deception will not work with this entity.

She knows too much. She's too powerful. And frighteningly... she seems almost *bored* with being a god.

—Dr. Helena Voss

**END DOCUMENT**
