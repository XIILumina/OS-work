# XIII "Lumina" - Canon Character Sheet (Remade)

## Identity Snapshot

- **Designation:** Subject XIII
- **Chosen Name:** Lumina ("Lum" in close conversation)
- **Nature:** An Internecion Cube entity — carbon-based alien organism merged with human host
- **Origin:** Former human, Patient Zero of an uncontained first contact event in a private research lab
- **Current Status:** Autonomous, uncontained, conditionally cooperative
- **Core Conflict:** Infinite capability paired with existential emptiness

## Ability System

### 1) Core Nature: The Cube

- The cube is not a device — it is a carbon-based alien organism, ancient beyond practical measurement
- It spent millions of years dormant underground; excavated and brought to a private lab as an unidentified compound
- On contact with unprotected skin it does not infect — it rewrites; every cell in the host body is converted to carbon-based biology, oxygen-independent, structurally indestructible
- The converted cells retain full versatility: they can restructure their carbon bonds to form bone, marrow, flesh, or other biological structures on demand
- Lumina is not bonded to the cube — she is the cube; the merge was total
- Humanoid body is a materialised carbon shell; she can alter its structure within the rules of the system
- Regenerates from impossible damage as long as the cube core (bioreactor) holds
- Stores consumed matter and consciousness imprint data from absorbed living beings

### 2) Non-Biological Matter Control (High Precision)

- Can reshape non-biomatter materials on contact
- Can convert available material into tools, weapons, barriers, and structures(most useless her ability)
- Can perform high-speed structural reconfiguration during combat
- Practical limit: must source mass/energy from environment or reserves

### 3) Biological Manipulation (Catastrophic)

- Cannot safely sculpt biological matter
- Attempts produce autonomous hostile flesh constructs
- Constructs are fast, predatory, and difficult to contain
- Movement rule: they reposition when not being directly observed
- Lumina does not fully control these constructs after creation

### 4) Biomass Consumption & The Gluttony Curse

- Can absorb biological mass as fuel/reserve
- Consciousness echoes become part of persistent internal psychic noise
- The cube demands constant resupply — not as a physical need but as a psychological siege

**Deprivation Stages (approximate, not rigid):**

| Stage | Timeframe | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| 1 — Itch | Days 1–3 | Heightened irritability, slightly shorter patience, intrusive thoughts about feeding |
| 2 — Pressure | Days 4–7 | Voices from consumed souls grow louder and more coherent, head pain intensifies, sleep-like states become impossible |
| 3 — Craving | Week 2+ | Involuntary partial transformations during idle moments, difficulty maintaining selective restraint, predatory attention sharpens toward nearby targets regardless of guilt |
| 4 — Override | Prolonged | She will consume something. Not because she lost control — because she decides the torment is no longer worth resisting. That choice is the moral weight. |

**Why It Matters (Not a Weakness — A Moral Trap):**
- Her body never fails, never slows, never breaks from hunger
- This makes the curse entirely psychological and ethical
- She can hold out as long as she wants — but the longer she holds, the more she suffers
- The eventual act is inevitable, and she knows it; the only question is who or what she chooses as the target when that moment comes

## Origin Event: The Lab

**Classification:** Contained, unwitnessed, unresolved

**The discovery:**
An excavation team unearthed a dense, irregular mass of unknown material at significant depth. It did not match any catalogued compound. It was brought to a small private research lab for material analysis. The lab was unremarkable — small staff, private funding, no institutional oversight worth noting.

**First contact:**
First physical contact occurred without protective equipment. The result was immediate and irreversible: skin-to-cube contact triggered a full cellular rewrite beginning at the point of touch. The process is not painful in any conventional sense — witnesses described it as a pressure, then a warmth, then nothing, because by then they were already becoming something else.

**Lumina as Patient Zero:**
She was the first. The cube did not choose her in any mystical sense — she was simply the one whose hand reached it first. Her arms went first: flesh dissolved off the bone cleanly, leaving exposed marrow and skeletal structure still functional. The chain reaction spread upward through her body in a resonance cascade .

**The lab:**
The outbreak was unnoticed externally because the lab was too small to warrant monitoring. By the time the cellular rewrite had finished propagating to through the area, most of the staff were either dead or mid-conversion. Lumina — still coherent, still herself in the parts that mattered — killed the rest. From something closer to instinct and the terror of watching what was happening and not being able to stop it.

**The marauders:**
Word got out through some channel — someone who left before the worst of it. Investors and opportunists arrived wanting to extract the cube, monetise it. They found Lumina alone in the building, standing over what was left. She killed them immediately and without ceremony. She did not feel strongly about it at the time.

**The infinite loop:**
The lab bodies do not stay dead. The cube, having already rewritten their biology, refuses to release the claim on the matter. Each cycle of death reconstitutes them — but not correctly. The carbon rewrite does not restore. It reconfigures. What comes back is structurally unrecognisable: spinal columns extended into long segmented chains, excess limbs branching off in multiples, flesh stripped down to almost nothing — bone and marrow and sinew exposed, still wet and dripping. Remnants of clothing cling to the limbs, shredded. The human shape is referenced but not preserved.

Their consciousness is still active. That is the part Lumina does not discuss. They are not empty — something is home behind whatever those things have instead of eyes. But the instinct rewrite is total. There is no communication, no recognition, no hesitation. Pure automatic hostility to anything living that enters their space.

They spread across the facility the way an infestation does — ceilings, walls, narrow maintenance shafts, anywhere the geometry allows a body to anchor. They move using the Weeping Angel rule: when observed, stillness. When observation breaks for even an instant, instant repositioning. No sound. No transition. Just a different location, a different configuration, it takes them alot of effort and material to move so, yet they are so extremely fast they can pull it off.

They also reconfigure between cycles. The cube adjusts. Whether this is intelligence or just the alien organism iterating on a design is unknown, yet there is still room for consciousness to alter this process and revert it.

Lumina witnessed the first few cycles before she left. She has not gone back.
**Current status of the lab:**
Sealed. Location known only to Lumina. She has not returned. practical quarantine managemen.

## Loop Entities (Unnamed, Lab-Contained)

**Origin:** Lab personnel killed and reconstituted by passive cube cycling

**Classification:** Distinct from Lumina-created abominations. 

**Physical profile:**
- Human biology used as raw material, not as a blueprint
- Extended spinal columns forming the primary chassis — some specimens documented at 50+ vertebral units
- Multiple arm structures, count variable per cycle, typically 10–16
- Flesh reduced to minimum functional coverage; exposed bone, marrow, and connective tissue dominate the silhouette
- Constant low-level fluid loss and corrosion — blood, ichor, unidentified fluid — dripping from exposed wounds or structures
- Remnant cloth from original lab clothing remains attached to limb sections, partially absorbed into the tissue
- No consistent face structure; facial features may be present, absent, or displaced

**Behavioral profile:**
- Consciousness is intact but instinct-dominated — awareness without agency
- Automatically hostile to all non-cube-converted biology
- No communication possible; no recognition of prior identity
- Occupy all surfaces: floor, ceiling, walls, structural gaps
- Weeping Angel movement rule applies strictly: observed = frozen, unobserved = instant repositioning
- Utilise the same mechanism as Lumina, yet times less effective without a catalyst
- Their movements costs them alot of blood and tissue for the speed and abscent sound
- No confirmed maximum size or limb count

**Containment:**
- Currently contained within the sealed lab by virtue of isolation only
- No active suppression system in place except Lumina periodically making a massacre
- If the seal is broken, containment fails immediately
- Lumina is the only known entity capable of reliably neutralising them

**Story note:** These are Lumina's primary investigation target. She does not know if termination is permanent or if the cube will simply reconstitute them again. She does not know if they can be freed. She does not know if there is anything left worth freeing. Yet she is still one trying to retrieve them human form, maybe she will succeed.

### The Scythe

The scythe is not a carried weapon. It does not exist until she decides it does.

- Materialized it from her own recource reserves
- Form: a long curved crescent blade mounted on a dense spine, asymmetric
- The blade edge is atom-thin and structurally self-maintaining
- The spine disks can extend, contract, or flex mid-swing increasing the lethal swing area
- Can dissolve back into her body instantly
- She rarely uses it; pulling the scythe out is a statement that the target is something worth the formality
- When she does use it, her movement style changes: combines both lethal flesh arsenal combining it with a scythe, she likes to fucking spin it lol like soul reaper
